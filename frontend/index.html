About the Project

This project provides a smart system to collect and segregate electronic waste generated on campus. It combines smart bins, real-time weight monitoring, and automated classification to improve recycling rates and recover valuable materials.

  • What is e-waste: Discarded electronic devices and components.
  • Why IIT Mandi generates e-waste: Labs, student devices, and research equipment lead to routine e-waste streams.
  • Why management is needed: Prevent hazardous disposal, recover materials, and reduce carbon footprint.

Problem Statement

  • Improper e-waste disposal leading to environmental harm.
  • Lack of reliable classification and tracking systems.
  • No easy way to plan timely collections from bins.
